Choose between safety and profitability, or find a balance between both aspects

Thanks to the processing of open data provided by the US government, you can know the best places to make your university experience much better, considering the following aspects:



Affordability

Have access to the prices of the best rental sites for you and select the one that best fits your budget.



Safety

Find the safest sites in Chicago according to the processing of information regarding the city's public safety situation and the police stations closest to them.



Recreation

Do you want to spend good moments of rest and relaxation? Look for rental options near parks in the city where you can spend time with your friends and have fun.



Traffic

Select places in areas with a good mobility of traffic, that way you will save valuable time and will be able to arrive in a short time to any destination from there.
